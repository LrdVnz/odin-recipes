Let's do carbonara !.

Description

"Spaghetti alla carbonara" is a classic Italian dish, originating from Rome. It is quick, easy and tasty to do.

Ingredients:

  • 100 grams of Pasta (Spaghetti is preferrable)
  • 3 eggs
  • 50 grams of Guanciale
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Put the spaghetti pasta in the water for 10 minutes
  3. in the meanwhile, prepare a bowl, and break the eggs into it.
  4. Mix the eggs until you get an uniform creme, add salt and pepper as you like Some add even a bit of milk.
  5. When the pasta is ready, throw it into a pan
  6. throw the egg creme with the pasta, and also the guanciale, mix together for minute
  7. Be careful not to let the creme solidify too much : it just has to not be entirely liquid.
  8. Enjoy !
